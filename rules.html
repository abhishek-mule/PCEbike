Rules & About

Rules & Terms

Your guide to a safe and enjoyable cycling experience

Eligibility & Documentation

  • Must be a registered college student
  • Valid college ID mandatory
  • Maintain active student status
  • Complete safety orientation

Timing & Duration

  • Operating hours: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Maximum rental: 4 hours/day
  • Early return bonus points
  • 24-hour advance booking

Safety Protocols

  • Helmet is mandatory
  • Follow traffic signals
  • No passenger riding
  • Regular safety checks

Usage Guidelines

  • Stay within campus limits
  • Use designated bike lanes
  • No off-road adventures
  • Report route changes

Maintenance & Care

  • Report damages immediately
  • Clean after use
  • Lock properly when parked
  • Regular maintenance checks

Fees & Penalties

  • Security deposit: ₹1000
  • Late return: ₹50/hour
  • Damage charges as assessed
  • Monthly subscription available

Terms of Service

By using PCEpark services, you agree to follow all safety guidelines and usage policies. The service is exclusively for college students and staff. Users are responsible for the bike during their rental period. Violation of rules may result in suspension of privileges and additional charges. PCEpark reserves the right to modify these terms at any time.
