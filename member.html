Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for membership?
Simply choose your preferred plan, complete the online registration form, and submit the required documents. Our team will verify your information within 24 hours.
Can I change my membership plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership plan at any time. Changes will take effect from the next billing cycle.
What's included in the insurance coverage?
Our basic insurance covers accidents and third-party liability. Premium plans include additional coverage for personal injuries and enhanced bike damage protection.
How do I cancel my membership?
You can cancel your membership anytime through your account settings or by contacting our support team. A 30-day notice is required for cancellation.
